Contact
=======

If you would like to get in touch with the Open Water Lab (OWL) team, please
use one of the following channels.

|github-icon| GitHub
------
For issues, feature requests, or documentation-related questions:

`OpenWaterLab on GitHub <https://github.com/OpenWaterLab>`_

|email-icon| Email
-----

For general inquiries or collaboration opportunities:

``openwaterlab@ugent.be``

|institution-icon| Institutional affiliation
-------------------------

Open Water Lab (OWL) is developed within the `BIOMATH research group <https://biomath.ugent.be>`_ at `Ghent University <https://ugent.be>`_.
