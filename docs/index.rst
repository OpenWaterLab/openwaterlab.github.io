Open Water Lab (OWL)
====================

Open Water Lab (OWL) is founded, developed and maintained by the `BIOMATH research group <https://biomath.ugent.be>`_ research group at Ghent University and the wider open-source water research community. It is a free and open-source ecosystem of tools for water system modelling, simulation, analysis, and digital twin development. Contributions of all kinds are welcome and appreciated.
