# OpenWaterLab (OWL)

OpenWaterLab (OWL) is is an ecosystem of free and open-source software for water systems modelling, simulation and analysis.

- Organization: https://github.com/OpenWaterLab  
- BIOMATH research group: https://github.com/UGentBiomath  

## Packages
- **owl-data** — data utilities for water/wastewater systems  
- **owl-twin** — reusable pipelines for digital twins of water systems  
- **owl-sim** — modelling and simulation tools for water systems.
